Child Care Roles

Diverse opportunities to support children's learning and development

Preschool Teachers

Qualified teachers developing and implementing educational programs for children aged 0-5 years.

  • $35-42 per hour
  • University qualification required
  • Curriculum development
  • Professional development support

Child Care Educators

Support children's learning and development through play-based activities and care routines.

  • $28-35 per hour
  • Certificate III/Diploma pathways
  • Hands-on learning environment
  • Career progression opportunities

Room Leaders

Lead age-specific rooms and mentor junior educators while maintaining high-quality care standards.

  • $32-38 per hour
  • Leadership development
  • Mentoring responsibilities
  • Quality improvement focus

Child Care Requirements

Essential qualifications and attributes for child care professionals

Qualifications & Checks

  • Certificate III in Early Childhood Education and Care (minimum)
  • Working with Children Check
  • First Aid, CPR, and Anaphylaxis training
  • Police clearance certificate

Personal Qualities

  • Genuine love for working with children
  • Patience, creativity, and enthusiasm
  • Strong communication teamwork with children and families
  • Cultural sensitivity & inclusiveness

Professional Skills

  • Understanding of child development principles
  • Ability to plan and implement learning activities
  • Observation and documentation skills
