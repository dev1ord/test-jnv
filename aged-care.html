Australia's aged care sector is experiencing unprecedented growth, creating thousands of rewarding career opportunities for compassionate professionals. With an aging population and increasing demand for quality care services, the industry offers stable employment, competitive salaries, and the chance to make a real difference in people's lives.

Registered Nurses

Lead clinical care teams and provide specialized nursing services in residential and community settings.

  • $45-60 per hour
  • Leadership opportunities
  • Continuing education support
  • Flexible scheduling

Personal Care Workers

Provide direct personal care and support to residents in their daily activities and wellbeing.

  • $28-35 per hour
  • Certificate III training provided
  • Part-time and full-time options
  • Career progression pathways

Care Coordinators

Manage care plans and coordinate services to ensure optimal outcomes for residents and families.

  • $28-35 per hour
  • Administrative and clinical blend
  • Professional development
  • Team leadership roles

What You Need

Essential qualifications and skills for aged care careers

Qualifications

  • Certificate III in Individual Support (Ageing)
  • First Aid and CPR certification
  • Working with Vulnerable People
  • English proficiency (IELTS/PTE)

Personal Qualities

  • Compassionate and patient nature
  • Strong communication skills
  • Physical fitness for hands-on care
  • Cultural sensitivity and respect

  • Flexible shift patterns available

  • Emotional resilience required
