Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions

What is JobsNVisa?

At JobsNVisa, we specialize in connecting businesses like yours with top-tier talent from across the globe. With a deep understanding of the international job market and a proven track record in staffing, we take the complexity out of hiring, ensuring you have access to the skilled labor you need to thrive.

How much do your services cost?

Our job placement services depends on the specific needs of each client. We offer competitive pricing and transparent fee.

Which industries do you specialize in?

We specialize in high-demand industries including Aged Care, NDIS, Healthcare, Construction, IT & Engineering, Residential Care, Retail, and Child Care. These sectors offer excellent career opportunities and are experiencing strong growth across Australia.

How do I apply for jobs?

You can apply for jobs by creating a profile on our website, uploading your resume, and applying directly to positions that match your skills and experience. Our team will review your application and contact you if you're a suitable match.

What documents do I need to provide?

Typically, you'll need an updated resume, relevant qualifications and certifications, identification documents, and any professional licenses. Specific requirements vary by industry and position.

Do you help with visa applications?

Yes, we provide comprehensive visa assistance through our licensed migration agents. We help with various visa types including skilled worker visas, employer-sponsored visas, and temporary work visas.

What visa types do you assist with?

We assist with Temporary Skill Shortage (TSS) visas, Employer Nomination Scheme (ENS) visas, Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme (RSMS) visas, and various skilled migration visas. Our migration agents will assess your situation and recommend the best pathway.

What support do you provide after job placement?

We provide ongoing support including settlement assistance, workplace orientation, professional development opportunities, and regular check-ins to ensure your success in your new role.

Do you offer training and certification programs?

Yes, we partner with registered training organizations to provide industry-specific certifications and training programs. This includes Certificate III qualifications for aged care and disability support, as well as other professional development courses.
