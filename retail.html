Retail Opportunities

Diverse roles across all retail environments and specialties

Sales Associates

Front-line customer representatives service helping customers find products and complete purchases.

  • $25-32 per hour
  • Flexible scheduling options
  • Sales incentives and bonuses
  • Product training provided

Leaders & Supervisors

Lead retail teams, manage daily operations, and ensure excellent customer service standards.

  • $30-38 per hour
  • Leadership development
  • Staff training responsibilities
  • Performance management

Stock & Inventory

Manage inventory, receive shipments, and ensure products are properly displayed and stocked.

  • $26-34 per hour
  • Early morning or evening shifts
  • Physical activity and variety
  • Inventory management systems

Cashiers & Customer Service

Process transactions, handle customer inquiries, and provide exceptional service at point of sale.

  • $25-30 per hour
  • Entry-level opportunities
  • Customer interaction focus
  • Technology training included

Getting Started in Retail

Essential requirements and skills for retail success

Basic Requirements

  • Right to work in Australia
  • Reliable transportation to work
  • Availability for retail hours (including weekends)
  • Professional presentation and grooming

Customer Service Skills

  • Friendly and approachable personality
  • Strong communication skills
  • Problem-solving abilities
  • Patience and empathy with customers

Technical Skills

  • Basic computer and POS system operation
  • Cash handling experience preferred
  • Willingness to learn new systems
  • Attention to detail and accuracy
